S M Chemicals

S M Chemicals is engaged in the manufacture of speciality water soluble Polymers for water purification and waste water purification for Sewerage water, effluent water and textile effluent colour removal polymers and dispersants for reverse osmosis water, boiler and cooling tower water treatment polymers. It is the largest textile effluent colour removal polymer and second largest in dispersants manufacturing in Telangana state. These polymers have wide application in water treatment like boiler cooling tower, water softening for textile, multiple effect evaporators, reverse osmosis plants and textile effluent colour removal,COD,BOD and TSS reduction in treated waters.

SM Chemicals Established in the year 1996 at Hyderabad, Telangana, we “ S M Chemicals” are a Sole Proprietorship (Individual) based firm, involved as the manufacturer, trader, retailer and wholesaler of a wide assortment ofwater treatment Chemicals, Benzalkonium Chloride, Cooling Tower Chemical and many more. These products are known for their unmatched quality at the logical price in the predetermined time period.
